About Us

About us

Inspired by the global television series, Chef Ramsay’s Hell’s Kitchen restaurants have become a fan-favorite in Las Vegas, serving over 1 million Beef Wellingtons since opening their doors in 2018. Today, Hell's Kitchen serves guests in Lake Tahoe, Southern California, and Atlantic City.

Hells Kitchen History

Originally a television series, host and Michelin-starred Chef Gordon Ramsay challenged a new group of talented chefs each season splitting them into the iconic red and blue teams in Hell's Kitchen. Facing challenges, dinner service and elimination, these hopeful chefs battle it out in the kitchen with hopes of becoming Chef Ramsay's protege. Nominated for six Emmy awards, Hell's Kitchen is currently in the midst of its 21st season.

Gordon Ramsay

Internationally renowned, multi-Michelin starred Chef Gordon Ramsay has opened a string of successful restaurants across the globe, from the UK and United States to France and Singapore. Gordon is also an international TV star, with shows that include Hell’s Kitchen, MasterChef, MasterChef Junior and Gordon Ramsay’s American Road Trip.
